Woven Badges

Woven badges offer a high-quality, durable, and detailed branding solution for businesses, organisations, and individuals. Created using fine threads woven together, these badges allow for intricate designs.

CHENILLE / FAUX-CHENIL

Chenille badges are a timeless and stylish choice, known for their soft, plush texture and bold, eye-catching appearance. Traditionally used on varsity jackets, letterman patches, and sports team apparel, chenille badges add a classic, high-end touch to any garment.

Biker Patches

Badges: A Symbol of Identity and Distinction

Badges are more than just decorative elements—they represent identity, achievement, and belonging. Whether used for branding, recognition, or personal expression.

Banners: Bold and Impactful Visual Communication

Banners are a powerful way to capture attention, convey messages, and enhance brand visibility. Whether for events, promotions, storefronts, or celebrations, banners provide a high-impact display that leaves a lasting impression.

Labels: Essential for Branding

Labels play a crucial role in branding, product identification, and consumer information. Whether used for packaging, apparel, or promotional materials, they enhance product presentation while providing key details such as brand names, care instructions, ingredients, or barcodes.

Clothing: Style, Comfort, and Expression

Clothing is more than just fabric—it's a form of self-expression, identity, and comfort. From everyday wear to high-fashion statements, the right apparel reflects personality, culture, and trends while offering functionality and style.

Epaulettes: A Mark of Authority and Prestige

Epaulettes are decorative shoulder pieces traditionally worn as symbols of rank, authority, or distinction. Commonly seen on military, aviation, and ceremonial uniforms, they add a touch of elegance and professionalism to formal attire.
