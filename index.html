About Us

We are manufacturers of high quality music stand banners based in Bristol, UK.

We supply music stand banners for bandstand display to all types of bands and choirs, from Symphony orchestras, through to individuals and not only in the UK but worldwide.

Every process in making our banners involves precision, skill and care to construct a music stand banner and we back that up with a fast and efficient customer service delivered by our friendly and professional team.

Each banner is designed, embroidered and manufactured in our Bristol based factory, with no minimum quantity orders. All enquiries welcome.

Head over to our main site www.badge-design.co.uk to find out more of what our company does.

OPTIONS

Because we make all our music stand banners in house you have a wide range of options. We will make banners to your specifications, such as fabric, colour, design, size and fringing.

APPLICATION

We offer three different application methods for our banners, embroidered, printed and appliquÃ©d.

Embroidered Elegance

This is our most commonly used type of design, it offers a higher quality finish that gives a premium look to your banners. Embroidered banners could use the applique technique, please see below for more info.

Printed Clarity

With print we are able to offer a higher level of clarity and complexity compared to our other two options. Print is used when the embroidery is too complicated. It is also our most cost-effective option.

AppliquÃ©

This is used when there is a large area of a embroidered logo that is a different colour to the fabric its going on. It is used to reduce the stitch count by using a piece of fabric, replacing the embroidered infill, thus reducing the price of production. This is only available for some designs.

FIXING

Solid Board Music Stand

With this stand, it would be best with our standard Envelope fixing. Extended envelope & Magnetic strip will also be suitable.* Please remember that not all music stands are magnetic.

Hollow Board Music Stand

With this stand, it would be best with our standard Velcro Loop fixing. There are two options for your Velcro positioning (see below). Magnetic strip will also be suitable.*

Envelope

Our Envelope Fixing ensures secure banner attachment, with Extended Envelope and Magnetic Strip options available for added flexibility.

Velcro

We recommend that any banners with a Velcro fixing would work best with a wooden dowel, it doesn't come with one but it is an optional extra. If you wish to have Velcro Loop, please choose your preferred Velcro option.

Our expertise in fixings allows us to produce various fixing styles and sizes to fit all stands.
We will need the dimensions of the stand.
Along with that we will need details about your stands so that we can accommodate.

Aperture

Not all stands need an aperture, but if your stand does then it is very important, this means we need to create a hole at the top of your banner so it can fit on your stand perfectly.

There are many different stands out there so not all apertures are the same. If you can measure the width of the metal protruding the top of the stand, that will be very useful.
