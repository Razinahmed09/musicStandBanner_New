OUR STORY

With 37 years in the automotive trade as an airbrush artist and custom painter, Gary Best has cultivated an extraordinary career fueled by passion, precision, and an unwavering love for cars. His unique skill set and dedication to the craft have earned him recognition in the car customization world and beyond.

Gary’s journey is marked by remarkable collaborations with local celebrities, including Julian from the Freekshow (Hott 99.5), R&B singer Ginuwine, WMAL radio talk host Mark Levin (Life, Liberty & Levin), and Rolling Thunder Bikes 2007 for DC's Harley Davidson dealer. In 2005, he crafted award-winning widebody imports for Team Emotion, which dominated East Coast car shows like “Hot Import Nights” with accolades such as Best Paint and Best of Show.

For over 20 years, Gary managed his own custom shop in Maryland, delivering cutting-edge designs that graced the pages of magazines like Sport Truck (1994), Super Street (1999), HCI (2001), Super Streetbike (2006), and Import Tuner (1999). His custom creations stole the spotlight at events such as Ocean City Car Shows, Tuner Wars, and World of Wheels, consistently earning titles like Best of Show, Best Muscle Cars, Best Imports, and Best Paint.

Evolving with the industry

Gary transitioned to a streamlined business model focused on providing exceptional service directly to customers. By integrating Carfax and insurance companies into his processes, he ensures clients are protected in case of accidents or mishaps. His extensive research led to certifications with top-tier brands like Feynlab, Ceramic Pro, IGL Coatings, Stek, and Nanopro, solidifying his expertise in ceramic coatings and PPF installations.

Pro Ceramic

Best Auto Salon, Gary prioritizes building generational customer relationships, placing trust and service above financial gain. His mission is clear: deliver unmatched quality, raise the bar in automotive care, and create a network of loyal clients who shine as brightly as their vehicles.

When you shine, we shine—making every investment in our company a shared success.

Our Mission

At Pro Ceramic - Best Auto Salon, our mission is to deliver exceptional automotive protection and enhancement services with unparalleled quality and care. We strive to build lasting relationships within our community by prioritizing professionalism, expertise, and customer satisfaction.

Our Goals

We aim to set the benchmark for excellence in automotive protection services, ensuring every vehicle receives top-tier care, from paint protection to ceramic coatings and beyond. By exceeding expectations with tailored solutions and adopting the latest technologies, we aspire to foster a loyal customer base and continually raise the industry standard.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the most trusted and sought-after name in automotive protection. Through our dedication to excellence, attention to detail, and passion for creating stunning, protected vehicles, we aim to redefine the automotive care experience and leave a lasting impression on every customer we serve.

Our Objectives

Our primary objective is to educate customers about the value and benefits of automotive protection services, empowering them to make informed decisions. We aim to expand our offerings and reach while consistently maintaining the highest standards of quality and care. By focusing on creating a seamless, enjoyable experience from start to finish, we ensure customer satisfaction at every step. Upholding our reputation with integrity, transparency, and superior craftsmanship remains at the core of everything we do.

Financial Benefits

Our services have the potential to significantly increase the appraisal value of your vehicle, making it a strong selling point. This enhancement is documented on your Carfax report history, providing tangible evidence of the added value our services bring. With this information readily available to potential buyers, your vehicle stands out as a more desirable option in the market, ultimately leading to a higher resale value and a smoother sales process.

5-9 Warranty Programs

We offer flexible programs based on how long you plan to keep your vehicle. For those who typically hold onto a car for 4-5 years, we recommend our 5-year program. If you expect to keep it longer, our 9-year program may be the best option, providing extended protection and value for your investment.

Quick Turnaround

Our impressive 48-72 hour turnaround time minimizes inconvenience for our customers, allowing them to get back on the road with their newly coated vehicle in a short amount of time. We understand the importance of efficiency and strive to deliver prompt service without compromising on quality. With our quick turnaround, you can enjoy the benefits of our coatings sooner and with minimal disruption to your schedule.
